About Bella Vista

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the rossi family, bella vista has serving authentic italian cuisine for over three decades. Our passion for traditional recipes and fresh ingredients has been made us a beloved destination for food lovers.

Every dish is prepared loved and attention to detail, using passed down througn generations of Italian chefs.
